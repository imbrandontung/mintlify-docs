## 中文版

### 為什麼選 100 天挑戰？

* **強迫每日輸出**：對抗 50+ 工作族群「想學但抽不出大塊時間」的最大敵人
* **結構化學習路徑**：避免在 YouTube / 部落格東看西看、結果哪個都沒搞懂
* **公開承諾**：把進度寫成 Portfolio = 給未來的自己一個「無法假裝沒進步」的紀錄

## English Version

### Why a 100-day challenge?

* **Forces daily output** — beats the biggest enemy of mid-career learners: "I want to learn but can't carve out a big block"
* **Structured path** — avoids the trap of jumping between YouTube/blogs and ending up understanding none of them
* **Public commitment** — writing the progress as a portfolio = giving future-me a record that "no, you can't pretend you didn't progress"
